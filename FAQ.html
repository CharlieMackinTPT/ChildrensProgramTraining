  • Account

  • What is the Mashopolis Children Program?

    Mashopolis is a new Twin Cities PBS television series and outreach program with a mission to provide children and families with tools to enhance their executive function skills and knowledge about diverse career options, to prepare them for future careers. This innovative program combines the power of storytelling, interactive media, intergenerational learning and equity to engage children ages 5-8 in bulidng the critical skills they need to join-and lead-the 21st century workforce.

  • How do I create my account?

    To create your account, go to https://bit.ly/MashopolisPlatform. Select the “Grown-Ups” button and follow the instructions. To watch step-by-step instructions, go to the Mashopolis training for teacher website https://bit.ly/MashopolisTrainingForTeacher.

  • How do I create a child account?

    To create accounts for your children, you will need to create a new group in your personal account. Click on the “Create New Group” button and name your group. Once your group is created, input the number of users you want. The platform will generate the QR codes and passcodes for each child. Children will name their accounts when they login, but you have the option to edit the names. To watch step-by-step instructions, go to the Mashopolis training website https://bit.ly/MashopolisTrainingForTeacher.

  • What if I have multiple classrooms that are doing the Mashopolis program?

    If you have multiple classrooms doing the program, you will need to generate multiple groups. Follow the instructions to creating accounts for children. Watch step-by-step instructions, go to the Mashopolis training website https://bit.ly/MashopolisTrainingForTeacher.

  • How do I print the QR codes or passcodes for my children?

    After creating children accounts, you can print the QR codes and passcodes by clicking on the QR code icon, or the “Print” button. To watch step-by-step instructions, go to the Mashopolis training website https://bit.ly/MashopolisTrainingForTeacher.

  • How do children log in to their accounts?

    Children will log in to their account on the Mashopolis Platform homepage https://bit.ly/MashopolisPlatform. They will select “Children’s Login” and use their QR code or passcode to sign-in. To watch step-by-step instructions, go to the Mashopolis training website https://bit.ly/MashopolisTrainingForTeacher.

  • Platform

  • Which web browser do I use?

    The Mashopolis Platform is most compatible with Google Chrome.

  • Where do I find the pdf instructions for the activities?

    The pdf instructions for the Role Play Activity, Career Match Up, and Paper Game can be found in your Mashopolis Platform account. To view and print the instructions, watch our step-by-step instructional video at https://bit.ly/MashopolisTrainingForTeachers.

    • Tablets

    • What devices do I use?

      The Mashopolis Platform can be accessed through any device that’s connected to the internet. You can load and play the activities on a desktop computer, laptop, tablet, and phone! The Mashopolis Platform is most compatible with the Google Chrome web browser.

    • Resources

    • What training is available?

      To get trained on the Mashopolis Children Program go to our training for teacher website at https://bit.ly/MashopolisTrainingForTeacher. In this training you will learn about the program and activities.

    • What materials do I need?

      You must have internet access and a device to be a part of this program. The platform is compatible with most devices. All hands-on activities will require material preparation. It is recommended that you view the material list on the pdf instructions before implementing.
